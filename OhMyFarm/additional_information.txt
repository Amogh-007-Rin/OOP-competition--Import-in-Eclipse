============================================================
                    ANIMAL DATA FORMAT (INPUT)
============================================================

Each line in animals.txt follows the structure:

    <TYPE>,<NAME>,<AGE_MONTHS>

Where:
    <TYPE>        is one of MOUSE, DUCK, or PIG
    <NAME>        is the animal’s name
    <AGE_MONTHS>  is the animal’s age in months


============================================================
                     ANIMAL INTRODUCTION
============================================================

Every animal can make an introduction.

Each animal type uses its own introduction style. The style
differs in the way information is ordered and phrased, but each
must include:

    - the animal’s name
    - the correct label (baby or adult)
    - the correct sound
    - the age in months


============================================================
                METADATA FOR EACH ANIMAL TYPE
============================================================

------------------------------------------------------------
MOUSE
------------------------------------------------------------
Baby label:        pup
Adult label:       mouse
Adult threshold:   5 months
Baby sound:        squeak

    My sound is "<SOUND>". I am <NAME>, a <LABEL>.
    I am <AGE_MONTHS> months old.
